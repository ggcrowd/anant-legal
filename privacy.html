Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Anant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Locally

  • Naam Jap Counts: Your mantra counting data is stored locally on your device
  • Daily Statistics: Daily and lifetime tracking data stored locally
  • Custom Naams: Custom mantras you create are stored locally
  • Settings: Your preferences (language, goals, etc.) stored locally

2.2 Analytics Data (Optional)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve the experience. This is optional and can be disabled in Settings.

  • App opens and session duration
  • Feature usage (anonymized)
  • Crash reports (no personal data)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Storage: All your spiritual practice data stays on your device
  • Analytics: To improve app performance and user experience (optional)
  • E-books: Firebase Storage delivers spiritual texts to you

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Payment information
  • Social media data
  • Biometric data

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Analytics and e-book storage
  • Data: Anonymous usage statistics, crash reports
  • Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Data Security

  • All data stored locally on your device is protected by device-level encryption
  • Firebase connections use industry-standard HTTPS encryption
  • We do not transmit your practice data to any servers

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Opt-Out Analytics: Disable in Settings (coming soon)

7.2 Data Portability

Contact us to request an export of your analytics data (if any)

8. Children's Privacy

Anant is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Compliance

  • India: Information Technology Act 2000, Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023
  • International: GDPR-compliant for users outside India

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
