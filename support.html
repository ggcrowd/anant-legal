Common Questions

Q: How do I delete my data?
A: Simply uninstall the app. All data is stored locally on your device.

Q: Is the app really free?
A: Yes! Anant will always be 100% free with no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Q: Can I request features?
A: Absolutely! Email us or create a GitHub issue with your suggestions.

Q: Why doesn't the app have my favorite mantra?
A: You can easily add custom mantras in the Settings screen!

Q: Does the app work offline?
A: Yes! All core features work offline. You only need internet for e-books (first download).

Q: How do I change the language?
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Language and choose between Hindi and English.

Q: My count was lost, can I recover it?
A: Unfortunately, data is stored locally. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, counts cannot be recovered. We recommend regular backups by taking screenshots of your stats.

Q: Can I sync my data across devices?
A: Not currently. Data is stored locally for privacy. Cloud sync may be added in future updates.
